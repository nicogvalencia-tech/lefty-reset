### Usage Guide

Rules:
1. Personalize every DM — at minimum with the brand name. For warm outreach (DM 3), open with a genuine specific reference to the brand before anything else. For cold outreach (DMs 1 & 2), leading with your stats is the hook — that's fine.
2. Send all DMs from @nicothelefty on Instagram (most active, highest follower count).
3. The first DM never pitches packages or investment — its only goal is to get a reply.
4. If no reply after 5–7 days, send DM 4 once. Then move on.
5. Never include pricing, tiers, or package details in a DM — save that for email or a call.
6. Keep every DM to 4–5 sentences max. Conversational, never salesy.

When a Brand Replies:
1. Reply same day — ideally within the hour.
2. Send the sponsor deck PDF and the full sponsorship package menu.
3. Include a soft call offer: "Happy to walk you through it on a quick call too — here's my Calendly: https://calendly.com/nico-nomadpickleball/15min"
4. If no reply to the deck after 5–7 days, send one brief follow-up email (not another DM).

Suggested Outreach Order:
1. Warm leads first (brands Nico has used, tagged, or mentioned) — use DM 3
2. Pickleball-specific brands — use DM 1
3. Health & fitness brands — use DM 2
4. Follow up on all non-replies at day 5–7 — use DM 4

Target Brand Categories:
- Pickleball paddle brands
- Pickleball apparel & footwear
- Sports nutrition & supplements
- Recovery tools (foam rollers, massage guns, etc.)
- Healthy food & beverage brands
- Fitness equipment brands
- Health tracking apps & wearables
