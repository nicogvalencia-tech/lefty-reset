## PRE-LAUNCH WEEK 1 — Announce & Build Hype

**Goal:** Make noise. Get people curious. Drive to registration page.

### Instagram
1. **Announcement post** — Bold graphic or video. "Something big is coming. 60 days. $1,000. And I need YOU."
   - Caption: "I've been working on something for this community for a while now. It drops [DATE]. Follow along. 👀 #LeftyTransformation #LeftyReset"
2. **Teaser Reel** — Nico on the court, energetic. "What if 60 days could change everything? Stay tuned."
3. **Prize reveal post** — Graphic showing $1,000 grand prize. "Grand Prize: $1,000 cash. Free to enter. Details dropping [DATE]."

### TikTok
1. **Teaser video** — Fast-paced, punchy. "POV: You're about to join the most fun fitness challenge in pickleball. Coming [DATE]."
2. **"Would you do this?" hook** — "Would you do a 60-day challenge for $1,000? Drop a 🎯 if you're in."

### Facebook
1. **Announcement post** — Share the teaser with a longer caption explaining what's coming. Tag your pickleball community groups.
2. **Personal story post** — Nico shares why he created the challenge. Authentic, conversational.

### Long-Form Content
**Reel/Video:** "Why I Created the Lefty Reset" — Nico talks directly to camera. Personal, real, vulnerable. Why fitness matters to him, why he's putting up $1,000, and why this community is the right place for it.

Not live yet — use this week to finalize community setup.

## CONTENT PRODUCTION TIPS

- Batch film talking-head videos weekly (1 session = 2–3 videos)
- Use Stories daily for codewords and check-in reminders — low effort, high reach
- Repurpose every TikTok as an Instagram Reel
- Have your VA schedule Facebook posts 1 week in advance
- Always have the check-in link (nicothelefty.com/checkin) in bio and every relevant post
